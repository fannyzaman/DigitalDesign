Down the Rabbit Hole 2024

This script demonstrates how distributed systems might handle Byzantine Fault scenarios.

In this simulation, the nodes attempt to reach consensus between: Red and Green.

When Red reaches consensus they attack.

When Green reaches consensus they retreat

When they reach No Consensus they don't act.
